Mission

The Columbia University College Republicans is a political organization and chartered student group at Columbia University, formed to promote ideas and positions of the Republican Party and ideological conservatism, foster community amongst Republican and conservative students on campus, and provide support for the political activities of Republican and conservative students on campus.

Events

We regularly host speaker events, social mixers, and dinners with sympathetic faculty members. In addition, we meet on a regular basis to discuss topics in politics and current events. We are open to all perspectives, and are one of the most ideologically diverse groups on campus. Join our mailing list to be kept up to date on our schedule.

Deputy Program

CUCR deputies shadow officer board members, attend officer meetings and give input into the direction of the organization. It serves as an opportunity for members to get mroe involved in the club, and new students to take an active role in contributing to campus life. We are currently accepting applications for the next class of deputies.
